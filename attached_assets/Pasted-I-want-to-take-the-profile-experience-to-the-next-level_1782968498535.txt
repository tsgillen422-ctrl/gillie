I want to take the profile experience to the next level and make it feel more like a polished social app (similar to Facebook or Instagram), while still making it unique to Gillie. The goal is to make people want to show off their profile and boat.

1. Redesign the profile header

Keep the current cover photo and profile picture, but make the top of the profile feel more premium.

* Cover photo
* Profile picture
* Name and username
* Badges
* Stats (Posts, Followers, Following, Favorites)
* Show current status:
    * 🟢 On the lake at Sunset Marina
    * 📍 Last checked in…
    * ⚪ Offline

Instead of just a “Find Me on Map” button, make the status feel alive.

⸻

2. Add a “My Boat” card (largest feature)

Directly below the profile header, add a dedicated boat card.

The card should include:

* Large boat photo (uploaded by the user)
* Boat name
* Boat brand
* Boat model
* Boat type
* Year
* Boat color
* Home marina (optional)

Example:

🚤 Lake Therapy

Fountain 38 Lightning

Performance Boat

2019

Deep Blue

Sunset Marina

The boat photo should become one of the highlights of every Gillie profile.

⸻

3. Boat photo uploads

Allow users to upload a photo of their actual boat.

Instead of only displaying an icon everywhere, use:

* actual boat photo on profile
* boat photo in boat details
* optional boat photo when someone taps their profile

Boat icons should still be used on the map for clarity, but profiles should showcase the real boat.

⸻

4. Support users who DON’T own boats

Gillie shouldn’t feel like it’s only for boat owners.

Add a toggle in Boat/Vessel Details:

☐ Display my boat on my profile

or

☐ I don’t own a boat

When enabled:

Show the My Boat card.

When disabled:

Hide the entire boat section.

The user can still:

* check in
* post
* follow friends
* upload photos
* earn badges
* appear on the map

If they purchase a boat later they can simply turn it back on.

⸻

5. Make Gillie for all lake lovers

Even without a boat, users should still personalize themselves.

Instead of forcing Boat Details, allow them to customize:

Favorite Activities

• Boating

• Wakeboarding

• Tubing

• Fishing

• Kayaking

• Paddle Boarding

• Swimming

• Cliff Jumping

• Camping

• Hiking

• Sunset Cruises

• Water Skiing

This makes Gillie welcoming for everyone who enjoys the lake.

⸻

6. Improve the Friends section

Instead of only showing avatars:

Show

Profile photo

Boat icon or boat photo

Boat type

This makes friends feel more connected to boating.

⸻

📸 Lake Memories

Boat photos

Instead of only:

Dropped a pin

Show richer activity like:

⸻

9. Modernize Posts

Instead of simple tabs:

Posts

Pins

Gallery

Use:

📰 Posts

📍 Check-ins

📸 Photos

The Photos tab should display a clean Instagram-style photo grid.

⸻

10. Long-term personalization

Eventually I’d love users to personalize their boats even more by adding:

* Boat brand
* Boat model
* Boat nickname
* Boat year
* Boat colors
* Engine brand
* Horsepower
* Length
* Home marina
* Favorite lake
* Favorite cove
* Favorite activity
* Upload multiple boat photos

The goal is to make every Gillie profile feel unique and make people excited to show off both themselves and their boat.

Overall, I want the profile to feel more premium, more social, and more lake-focused—something people open and immediately think, “Wow, I want my profile to look like that.”
