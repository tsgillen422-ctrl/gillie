Gillie – “Today on the Lake” Vision

I want to make Gillie the most fun and engaging lake app available. I don’t want to copy Snapchat, Instagram, or Facebook, but I do want to take the best ideas from each and make them unique to boating and lake life.

🌊 Today on the Lake

I want to add a new feature called Today on the Lake.

This should be Gillie’s version of Stories.

When users open Gillie they immediately see what’s happening around the lake right now.

People should be able to post:

* Photos
* Videos
* Text updates

Stories automatically disappear after 24 hours.

Users should be able to post unlimited stories throughout the day.

⸻

Story Viewer

The viewer should feel smooth like Snapchat.

* Full screen
* Progress bars
* Tap left/right to skip
* Hold to pause
* Swipe down to close
* Auto advance

Examples:

📍 Sunset Marina

Example:

Users tap the location to watch everyone’s stories from there.

This makes the map feel alive.

Overall Goal

Gillie should become the first app people open before going to the lake.

I want users to think:

“I wonder what’s happening at the lake today.”

instead of opening Facebook, Instagram, or Snapchat.

The app should feel alive, exciting, and built specifically for lake communities. Every feature should reinforce that identity and make people want to share their boating adventures directly in Gillie.
