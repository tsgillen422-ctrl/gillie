I’d like to add a major new feature called “Today on the Lake.” I don’t want it to be a copy of Snapchat, but I want it to feel just as fun and addictive while being centered around boating and lake life.

Goal

When users open Gillie, they should immediately see what’s happening around the lake right now.

People should feel like they don’t want to miss anything that’s happening today.

⸻

Main Feed

Add a new section near the top of the Feed called:

🌊 Today on the Lake

This should display a horizontal row of story circles similar to Snapchat or Instagram Stories.

The first circle is:

➕ Add Story

Then show everyone’s active stories.

Stories disappear automatically after 24 hours.

Users can upload unlimited stories.

Supported story types:

* Photos
* Videos
* Text updates
* Location-tagged stories

⸻

Story Viewer

When someone taps a story:

* Full-screen experience
* Tap left/right to navigate
* Hold to pause
* Swipe down to close
* Auto advance
* Progress bar across the top

Very smooth and modern.

⸻

Location-Based Stories

Every story should automatically be associated with a location if the user wants.

Examples:

📍 Sunset Marina

📍 Willow Grove

📍 Sulphur Creek

📍 The Cliffs

📍 Party Cove

When someone taps these places on the map they can see every story posted there during the last 24 hours.

This makes the map feel alive.

⸻

Map Integration

On the map, active locations should display a glowing story ring.

Example:

Sunset Marina

🔵 24 Stories

The Cliffs

🔵 8 Stories

Party Cove

🔵 15 Stories

Tap location

↓

Watch stories from that location.

⸻

Feed Integration

Under Today on the Lake add:

🔥 Trending Today

Most active locations.

Examples:

🔥 Sunset Marina

24 Stories

🔥 Party Cove

18 Stories

🔥 Willow Grove

11 Stories

Users can tap to watch everything happening there.

⸻

User Profiles

Every profile gets a Stories tab.

People can watch that user’s stories from the past 24 hours.

⸻

Privacy

Respect existing privacy settings.

Friends only

Followers

Public

Blocked users

All current permissions should apply to stories.

⸻

Notifications

Optional notifications:

“Your friend Mike just posted from Sunset Marina.”

“15 new stories at Party Cove.”

“Sunset Marina is trending right now.”

⸻

Future Ideas

This system should be built so it can support:

* Reactions
* Replies
* Polls
* Music
* Stickers
* GIFs
* Mentions
* Event stories
* Business stories

without needing to redesign it later.

⸻

Design Goal

The experience should feel polished and exciting.

Think:

* Snapchat Stories
* Instagram Stories

combined with

* Live lake locations
* Boating
* Marinas
* Sandbars
* Friends
* Events

The objective is to make Gillie the first app people open before they head to the lake because they want to see what’s happening right now.

⸻

I have one addition I’d strongly recommend that isn’t in the mockup:

“Who’s Live”

If someone is actively checked in and posting stories, put a small LIVE badge on their boat icon and profile. Imagine opening the map and seeing five boats with LIVE badges—you instantly know there’s activity there. That ties your live map and stories together in a way that feels unique to Gillie, rather than like a generic social media feature.
